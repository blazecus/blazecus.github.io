Big Mode Jam 2026: Flockers
Game jam game made to fit the theme "slick"

I'm not going to post many screenshots here, go play the game instead, please!

This is the first real time I completed a jam on my own, and I was very proud of the result. It is heavily inspired by the flash game Mutiny in particular, which was itself inspired by Worms. Regardless, I think it plays really well, and I'm particularly proud of the animations I made, physics-based gameplay, and the simple but effective AI for the enemy penguins that I had to make in about an hour.
